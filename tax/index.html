What We take on

In scope

  • W-2 income, single or multiple employers
  • Self-employment income (Schedule C)
  • 1099-NEC / 1099-K contract and gig income
  • Single-state returns
  • Second state, only if it has no income tax

Out of scope

  • Multi-state returns with income tax in both states
  • Business entity returns (S-corp, partnership)
  • Complex investment or trust income
  • Prior-year amendments or IRS disputes
